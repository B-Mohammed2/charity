About Us

Helping Hands Charity is dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of homeless individuals and orphans across the United Kingdom. Our mission is to provide shelter, support, and education to those who need it most.

Our Activities

Shelter for the Homeless

We operate shelters in cities across the UK, providing a safe and warm place to sleep for those without homes.

Education for Orphans

We fund educational programs for orphaned children, ensuring they have access to quality education and a brighter future.
